underdawgs

a social web app for musicians

underdawgs is a social web application where musicians can showcase their music videos in a simple way. enthusiasts can at the same time discover new music and get inspired by the artists. i designed and co-developed this app by prototyping in sketch and then creating the app using php, xml, xslt, html, css and phpmyadmin as database.

date: november 2018
for: kth course xml for publishing
category: web design

memoar

days made clearer

memoar is a med-tech startup bringing ar-technology into the area of dementia health care by providing an intuitive software for ar-glasses. backed by researchers, dementia associations and with support from kth innovation, we are currently developing a prototype. working closely with the team and taking an idea from beginning to realisation have made me comfortable working with iterative processes and pushing the limit of creative thinking. apart from general business activities, i am mainly responsible for the product and design development, as well as marketing.

date: 2017 - present
for: voluntary/personal
category: entrepreneurial/ux/pr

chatMe

a location based chat app

chatme is an application where you can pick a chat buddy on a map and then start your conversation. you can also get suggested chat topics based on places nearby. i am currently designing and co-developing this app using react, socket.io, html, bootstrap, and css. google's api is used for positioning data.

date: march - april 2019
for: kth course interaction programming and the dynamic web
category: web design/development
